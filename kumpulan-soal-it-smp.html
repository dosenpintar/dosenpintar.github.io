Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

I had an unforgettable experience during  holiday in Lampung My family and I participate  Lampung Adventure Tour and we saw many  interesting things.

When we arrived at the park, a few elephants were ready to take us on our adventurous trip.  Accompanied by my guide, Mr. Dasa, we rode on  a gigantic elephant. We set off on a leisurely two-  hour trek through the surrounding jungle. We also  passed rice fields and streams. Along the way,  Mr. Dasa pointed at every imaginable spice, fruits  and vegetables. We saw coffee and cocoa trees.  

Two hours after we set out into the jungle, I saw  a mighty elephant hum drumming its way back to the  safari park with a cargo of Japanese tourists on its  back. Mr. Dasa and I made our way behind it and  followed it into the park.  It was a really unforgettable experience.
