PART 2
Temukan sinonim dari kata kerja dibawah ini :

1). Started = Mulai
=> Synonym : Begun, commenced.

2). Heard = Mendengar
=> Synonym : Listened to

3). Arrived = Tiba/sampai
=> Synonym : Reach, Visit

4). Looked = Melihat
=> Synonym : See, watch

5). Worked => Bekerja
=> Synonym : Processed, created.

PART 3
Susunlah menjadi kalimat yang baik

1). I was watching tv when they came.
Rumus yang digunakan adalah past continuous. Karena ada 2 kalimat, maka rumus nya :
....... (Past continuous)... When.... (Simple past)

Rumus past continous
S + To be (was/were) + V-ing

Rumus simple past
S + V2

2). Alina visited her big family in Jakarta last week.
Rumus yang digunakan adalah simple past :
S + V2 + O

3). I just got from yogyakarta.

Rumus yang digunakan adalah simple past :
S + V2 + complement

4). The next time we have friends over for dinner.

5). He was my classmate in elementary school.

Rumus nya adalah simple past nominal :
S + To be (was/were) + complement
