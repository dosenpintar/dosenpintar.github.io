2. what is recount text and make a simple example of recount text​


Jawaban:

A recount text is a piece of writing that narrates or retells past experiences or events. The primary purpose of a recount text is to inform, entertain, or share personal experiences with others. It typically follows a chronological order and includes five main components: orientation, events (sequence), description of participants or settings, evaluation or personal thoughts, and reorientation or closing.

Here is a simple example of a recount text:

Title: "A Weekend Trip to the Mountains"

Orientation:

Last summer, my family and I took a trip to the Rocky Mountains for a weekend getaway.

Events:

On the first day, we woke up early, packed our backpacks, and set out to hike the winding trails. As we ascended, we admired the picturesque views of the lush green valleys below.

We reached a beautiful waterfall after a few hours, where we decided to stop for a picnic lunch. We could feel the cool mist from the waterfall on our faces as we devoured our delicious sandwiches.

The next day, we explored a serene mountain lake. My brother and I rented a paddleboat, while my parents relaxed on the shore. We had a great time splashing around in the water and basking in the sun.

Evaluation:

Throughout the trip, I felt invigorated by the crisp mountain air, and we all enjoyed bonding during our outdoor adventure.

Reorientation:

As our weekend in the Rocky Mountains came to a close, we reluctantly packed up and headed back home, cherishing the memories we had made together.

In this recount text, the orientation sets the scene, the events are described in chronological order, the participants are identified, the author's personal thoughts are shared, and the reorientation provides a conclusion.

7. What is social function of recount text? And mention generig structure of recount text?​


Jawaban:

The social function of a recount text is to inform or entertain its audience, or both.

The text organization of a recount text is shown below.

a. Orientation, which gives the readers the background information necessary to understand the text, such as who was involved, what happened, when it happened, and where it happened.

b. Series of events, which retell the events of the person's life in the order in which they happened.

c. Reorientation, which serves as a personal comment about the series of events or what happened in the end (not always necessary).

Definition of Recount Text

Recount text is a text that telling the reader about one story, action or activity. Its goal is to entertaining or informing the reader. (sebuah teks yang menceritakan sebuah cerita, aksi ataupun aktifitas. Tujuan recount text adalah untuk menghibur atau memberi informasi kepada pembaca)

Or (atau)

Recount is a text which retells event or experiences in the past. (sebuah teks yang menceritakan kembali kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lampau)

type of recount text

Personal Recount Text

Ini merupakan teks yang menceritakan tentang sebuah cerita atau pengalaman pribadi seseorang, baik itu tentang perjalanan liburan, pengalaman yang tak terlupakan, maupun kisah pribadi lainnya.

Imaginative Recount Text

Ini merupakan teks yang menceritakan sebuah cerita yang didasarkan pada imajinasi pengarang dalam bentuk past tense, atau bisa juga disebut fantasi.

2. Factual Recount Text

Ini merupakan teks yang berfungsi untuk menyajikan laporan peristiwa yang benar-benar terjadi atau fakta nyata, seperti laporan percobaan ilmu pengetahuan ataupun laporan kepolisian.

3. Historical Recount Text

Ini merupakan teks yang didasarkan pada sejarah yang pernah terjadi pada dahulu kala. Cerita sejarah yang diceritakan kembali tersebut bisa berupa tempat bersejarah, benda bersejarah, maupun peristiwa-peristiwa sejarah yang mengiringinya. Salah satu contohnya seperti sejarah sumpah pemuda, sejarah didirikannya tugu monas dan masih banyak cerita sejarah lainnya.

Generic Structure of Recount Text

# Orientation : It gives the readers the background information needed to understand the text, such as who was involved, where it happened, and when it happened.

# Events : A series of events, ordered in a chronological sequence.

# Re-orientation :A personal comment about the event or what happened in the end.

Purpose of Recount Text

– To retell past experience

The Characteristics / Language Feature of Recount Text

– Using the simple past tense, past continuous tense, past perfect tense, and past perfect continuous tense.

– Using temporal sequence, e.g. On Saturday. On Monday, On Sunday

– Focus on specific participant, e.g. I (the writer)

– Using the conjunctions, such as: then, before, after, etc.

– Using action verd, e.g. went, stayed

Example of Recount Text

Being Late

Last morning, Dinar, my roommate woke up late and she had to go to campus.

When she wanted to take her motorcycle, in fact she couldn’t move it because there were some motorcycles that blocked up her motorcycle.

She tried to move all of the motorcycles, so that her motorcycle could move from the garage. But she couldn’t do it.

Then, she called Adel who had that motorcycle which blocked it up. After that, her friend who had that motorcycle helped her. Finally, she could move her motorcycle and rode it to go to campus.

Ketika dia ingin mengambil sepeda motornya, pada kenyataannya dia tidak bisa menggerakan motornya karena ada beberapa sepeda motor yang menghalangi sepeda motornya.

Dia mencoba untuk memindahkan semua sepeda motor itu, sehingga sepeda motornya bisa pindah dari garasi. Tapi ia tidak bisa melakukannya.

MACAM-MACAM RECOUNT TEXT

Recount text memiliki beberapa macam jenis teks yang bisa digunakan untuk Anda menulis. Disesuaikan dengan tujuan dari tulisan itu sendiri. Macam-macam jenis recount text bisa dilihat di bawah ini:

Personal recount. Personal recount adalah recount text yang berfungsi menceritakan tentang pengalaman pribadi penulis.

Contoh:

I spent my last summer in Seoul, South Korea. I went there by myself for a four days solo trip.

On the first day, I was landed at Incheon Airport around 7 AM after a 6 hours long flight. Then I went to Seoul by train and checked in to the hotel I already booked. I decided to take a rest for a while. At night, I went to Hongdae, a famous district in South Korea. I went to eat traditional Korean food and went around the neighbourhood. On the next day, I went to the Gyeongbokgung Palace and National Museum. I also went to learn how to make Kimchi and see the scenery of Seoul from Seoul Tower.  I went back to the hotel at 10 PM and immediately went to sleep.  On the third day, I went to the Korean Demilitarized Zone or DMZ. DMZ is a border barrier that divides South Korea and North Korea. I also went to Dora Observatory. From here, we can see how North Korea look like through binoculars.  I spent the 4th day walking around Myeondong and buy some stuff and souvenirs for my family and friends. I went to the airport at 3 PM since my flight is at 5 PM.  I have a fantastic experience in South Korea, especially get to see directly how the Korean Peninsula is. It's a great trip.

Factual recount . Factual recount adalah recount text yang berfungsi untuk menyajikan laporan peristiwa yang benar-benar terjadi, seperti laporan percobaan ilmu pengetahuan ataupun laporan kepolisian.

Contoh:

“Man Charged with Pushing Old Woman Down Bus"

SINGAPORE – The man who pushed an old woman down a bus along Upper Thomson Road has been charged in court.

Twenty-five-year-old Ong Kok Hao is accused of hurting 76-year-old Hwang Li Lian Nee Lye on bus service number 167 at about 3pm on June 5 this year.  Ong is said to have used his right hand to push the old woman on her back, causing her to fall on the steps of the bus.  The incident was captured in a two-minute video and uploaded on YouTube.  The video shows Ong suddenly flying into a rage at Madam Hwang for pressing the bell at the last-minute along Upper Thomson Road.  A shouting match then ensued and during the heated spat, Ong threatened to slap the woman, before pushing her down the bus.  Ong’s lawyer Eddie Koh will be making representations to the court. The case will next be mentioned in December.

Imaginative . Imaginative adalah jenis recount text yang berfungsi menyajikan sebuah cerita imaginatif. Lalu menuliskan peristiwa atau kejadian yang telah terjadi.

That was my bad experience with adolescence, though there were still lots of good experience too.

The Ant and the Dove

the generic structure :
Orientation : It gives the readers the background information needed to understand the text, such as who was involved, where it happened, and when it happened.
Events : A series of events, ordered in a chronological sequence.
Re-orientation :A personal comment about the event or what happened in the end.

30. recount text1. what's the social function of recount text2. what's the generic structure of recount text3. give me an example of recount​


Jawaban:

1. The social function of a recount text is to retell or narrate a series of past events or experiences, often in a chronological order. It is used to inform or entertain the audience by sharing personal experiences or factual accounts.

2. The generic structure of a recount text typically includes:

a. Orientation: This is where the writer introduces the context of the recount, such as who, what, where, when, and why.

b. Events: This section presents a chronological account of the events or experiences, usually in the order they occurred.

c. Reorientation: In this part, the writer may provide a closing statement or reflection on the recounted events.

3. Example of a recount:

"Last summer, my family and I went on a road trip to the Grand Canyon. We left on a bright and sunny morning and drove for hours. Along the way, we stopped at various landmarks and took in the breathtaking scenery. When we finally arrived at the Grand Canyon, the view was absolutely stunning. We spent the day hiking, taking pictures, and enjoying the natural beauty. As the sun began to set, we gathered around a campfire and shared stories. It was a memorable trip, and we hope to go back one day."
