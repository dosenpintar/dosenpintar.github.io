5. Tolong dijawab kak besok dikumpulkan, soal DLE kelas 10 Teknik Listrik​


Jawaban:

1. a. 1,2 ohm

b. 16,66A

2. 8 ohm

Penjelasan:

1. a.

1/Rtotal = 1/R1+1/R2+1/R3

1/Rtotal = 1/2+1/4+1/12

1/Rtotal = 6/12+3/12+1/12

1/Rtotal = 10/12

Rtotal = 12/10

Rtotal = 1,2 ohm

b.

i = V/Rtotal

i = 20/1,2

i = 16,66 A

2. Rparalel?

1/Rp = 1/R3+R4 + 1/R5

1/Rp = 1/6 + 1/12

1/Rp = 2/12+1/12

1/Rp = 3/12

Rp = 4

Rtotal = R1+R2+Rp

Rtotal = 2+2+4

Rtotal = 8 ohm
